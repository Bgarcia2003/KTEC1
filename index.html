Simple Ways to Keep the Earth Clean

Take small actions that make a big difference.

‚ôªÔ∏è Understanding Our Waste Impact

The United States generates approximately 292.4 million tons of municipal solid waste (MSW) annually, equating to about 4.9 pounds per person per day. This waste includes everyday items like packaging, food scraps, and household goods.

Recognizing the environmental impact, several states have enacted legislation to reduce plastic pollution. For instance, California's Senate Bill 1053, signed into law in 2024, bans all plastic shopping bags at grocery stores, effective January 1, 2026. Similarly, Colorado implemented a statewide ban on single-use plastic checkout bags and polystyrene foam containers for ready-to-eat food starting January 1, 2024.

These legislative actions demonstrate that policy changes can lead to significant environmental benefits. However, individual actions are equally important. Simple steps like using reusable bags, composting organic waste, and reducing single-use plastics at home can collectively make a substantial difference in minimizing our ecological footprint.
